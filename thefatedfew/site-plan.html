Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to keep a record of D&D campaigns done by my friend group. The site will hold information on the characters that were played, notes and recaps of sessions, recordings of sessions, animatics, and more.

Audience

The intended audience for this website if for those in my friend group, The Fated Few, to look back on all that we have done and have a proper place to keep good record of it. This can also be for any friends or family that want to see what we have been up to in our campaign.

Dynamic elements

Some of the dynamic elements in Java Scrips will include character sections that expand when clicked and change the color of parts of the website when hovered over. The session records page will have a search bar that allows users to quickly find a session based on keywords that appear in the notes and recap of that session.

Home page

Welcome to the lands of The Fated Few! We are a small group of friends who get together to play D&D and this website holds all the results of our shenanigans!
Our current, and first, campaign is the Odyssey Of Champions! Based in the mythical world of Theros, a D&D and Magic: The Gathering setting inspired by Greek mythology. Follow us on our journey to the end of the world!

Players:

  • Zach as the DM
  • Davin as Eragon
  • Emily as Cerein and Gavea
  • Jared as Ilias
  • Jonathan as Fatespinner and Thorok
  • Mathew as Kuul
  • Savannah as Lilthet
  • Tyler as Balazar

Characters:

Name: Lilthet Suncatcher (she probably had many nicknames but usually only family members or close friends know them, maybe eventually the party members)
Race and Class: Centaur; Cleric, lots of spells and being kinda bulky for a spellcaster it's kinda fun.
Background: She comes from a relatively small traveling band of centaurs full of merchants and some warriors to protect them. Huge family and knows quite a lot of random customs from different lands because of the travels. Known in her village as basically their chosen one and had a lot of pressure to live up to that image. This is because the god Heliod blessed/choose her at birth. She is now in this quest as basically a coming of age ceremony to show her who she is supposed to be and who she will become.
Alignment: Lawful Good; she strives to always do the right thing and shys away from unnecessary death. Loves to try to bring people together and wants to bring Sunshine to everyone she can!
Deity or Patron: Heliod; she technically didn't choose him, he choose her, but she believes in all that he stands for but thinks he can be pretty arrogant at times and can understand why others do not like him.
Personality Traits: Loyal to a fault, easy to trust but not stupid she won't let others get away with everything. Very emotionally driven.
Motivation: She is on her Coming of Age Journey to seek who she will become and to live up to her tribes expectations that she will be a renowned champion of Heliod.
Appearance: A really tall Clydesdale Centaur with a pale sandy colored coat. A long blonde braid for her hair and tail. Enjoys wearing light blues and yellows in her clothing. Many bags and packs since she cann carry so much. A large staff with Heliod symbols, other Heliod symbols on her necklace and clothing as well as wearing a laurel around her head.
Fun Fact: She feels super pressured almost constantly to keep people together and ensure success. Also weird Nyxborn stuff like her hair and eyes sometimes have a glow and her hair can sometimes float, as well as having birds chirp when she laughs.
One-Liner: (Optional) "Okay, how about we all take a seat and calm down to talk about this? I have the special talking stick!"

Name: Eragon, but he's been called a lot including metal man, construct, shiny individual, etc.
Race and Class: Warforged druid. Basically a batte medic, with a bit of transformer in there.
Background: While he's not 100% sure, he definitely remembers awakening In the forest then immediately called to serve nylea. Apparently was a creation of Daedalus.
Alignment: Neutral good; he always tries to do the right thing. However due to his lack of knowledge and affiliation with nylea he usual does what she asks without hesitation, and she does have beef with civilization so technically so does he.
Deity or Patron: If not obvious, Nylea.
Personality Traits: at first was very curious but now that he's been active for a while he's more serious. He tries to stay positive often attempting to boost morale any way he can. However he hasn't grown really attached to anything due to his sole focus of obeying Nylea.
Motivation: He wants to know what he is and why he was created. But more importantly he want to make to only person he's known proud (Nylea again).
Appearance: appearing to be a slender built warforged wearing tattered clothing. He's clearly seen some wear and is rusted all around. Wearing a necklace with 2 glowing green crystals and has recently developed patches of bark on him like armor and a large symbol of nylea on his chest.
Fun Fact: my dude is like a year old.
One-Liner: (Optional) "I have a really dumb idea."

**Name:** Cerein Serudath, previously Princess Cordelia Serudath **Race and Class:** Triton, Phantom Rouge. Darling is a literal agent of death. **Background:** Born as the Princess of Saphiren and the younger twin of her brother Faros. She grew up causing chaos along with her brother *and a certain someone,* until eventually her schemes became too dangerous. At 10 years old, she witnessed her brother die, narrowly escaping death herself, and left the kingdom behind due to the immense grief. More chaos. More death. At 15, her and her parents were killed (Consequences for your actions? What?). She was then chosen by Erebos to become his Nyxborn champion. **Alignment:** Lawful Evil, now that I think about it. **Deity or Patron:** The one and only, Erebos. **Personality Traits:** She cares for ~~almost~~ no one except herself, her family, and her kingdom. She is all too eager to kill but will always respect the dead. She keeps to herself and does everything she can to carry out the will of Erebos and seek his approval. **Motivation:** Her entire purpose in this second life is to obey Erebos' command and bring down his foes. However, she still has a soft spot for her home and family. **Appearance:** 5'1" Baby blue triton with large elegant fins that now fade to black. Her pitch black eyes sheen silver as they reflect nothing but darkness and death. She wears black clothes trimmed with silver and several belts, small chains, and small bags to carry several small items. She wears an inky black cloak that seems to blend into the shadows except for the symbol of Erebos on the right shoulder. She carries several weapons which are now often hidden by the cloak. **Fun Fact:** Due to being a Nyxborn: One can often see haunting black mist trailing behind her blades and other weapons as she uses them. Her scales give off a silver sheen under moonlight. *If* she screams or yells, the voices of long-gone souls seem to echo out and accompany her. And, when she laughs, all who can hear suddenly feel cold as a foreboding chill crawls up their spine. **One-Liner: (Optional)** "See you in the Underworld."

**Name**: Thorhok Thunukalithi. No nicknames... yet. **Race and Class**: Goliath Fighter, Cavalier subclass **Background**: After his parents disappeared into the mountains, and someone close to him left the clan, Thorhok set out to find his mother and father. Unfortunetly, his is a child, and so ended up nearly starving to death before Lilthet found him. Set off later to continue the journey to find his parents - Though we all know how *that* turned out. **Alignment**: I'll get back to you on that **Patron**: Iroas, god of Victory, as is normal for Goliaths. He has not really had much interaction with followers of other gods before this. **Personality traits**: Friendly and trusting to the point of nievete, curious, protective, very little respect for authority figures who have not proven themselves deserving of respect. Somewhat reckless and not always prone to considering the consequences of words and actions. Kind of dumb, but in a "I'm-a-ten-year-old-child-way-out-of-my-depth" kind of way. **Motivation**: Hopes to earn a nickname before his twelfth birthday, and strives to protect those he calls his own, as he wasn't able to in the past. **Appearence**: Bald, with bright blue eyes and a round, inviting face. Six foot seven inches, but suprisingly skinny, considering how powerful he is. His skin sparkles, as though diamonds had been embedded into it, or stars wrapped around him. **Fun fact**: Unlike most goliath males, Thorhok isn't naturally bald, instead shaving his head regularly. He is, of course, very insecure about this.
